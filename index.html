Happy Valentine's Day Bubba

My love, I don't know where to start because there's so much I want to tell you this Valentine's Day. But I'll try to express all my feelings for you through these words.

"Ikaw ang dahilan kung bakit ako ngumingiti nang walang dahilan. Ikaw ang nagpapainit sa puso ko kahit malamig ang panahon. Ikaw ang nagbibigay ng kulay sa mundo ko na dating puro black and white lang."

Tandaan mo pa ba yung unang beses tayong nagkita? Hindi ko malilimutan yung feeling na yun - parang bumagal ang oras at ikaw lang ang nakikita ko. Alam mo ba na simula noon, alam ko na special ka. Hindi ko inakala na magiging tayo, pero salamat sa Diyos at naging tayo nga.

Every day with you is a blessing. Yung simple moments lang natin together - yung mga kwentuhan natin, yung tawanan natin, kahit yung mga away natin (kahit na rare lang naman) - lahat yun special sa akin. You make ordinary days feel extraordinary just by being there.

"In a sea of people, my eyes will always search for you."

Salamat sa lahat ng ginagawa mo para sa akin. Salamat sa pagmamahal mo, sa suporta mo, sa pag-unawa mo, at sa pagiging patient mo sa akin kahit minsan sobrang kulit ko. Salamat sa pag-accept mo sa akin, kasama na yung mga flaws and imperfections ko.

You inspire me to be a better person every single day. Because of you, I want to work harder, dream bigger, and love deeper. Ikaw yung motivation ko sa lahat ng bagay. Kapag pagod na ako, iniisip ko lang na para sa future natin 'to, and suddenly I have all the energy in the world again.

"I love you not only for what you are, but for what I am when I am with you. You bring out the best in me, and I'm forever grateful for that."

Alam mo, ang dami kong pangarap para sa atin. Gusto kong makita tayong lumalaki together, nag-achieve ng mga dreams natin, nag-explore ng mundo, at nagtatayo ng sarili nating family someday. Hindi ko alam kung ano ang future, pero alam kong gusto kitang kasama sa lahat ng adventures na darating.

This Valentine's Day, gusto ko lang na malaman mo na you are loved beyond measure. You are cherished. You are appreciated. You are my best friend, my partner, my love, my everything. Wala akong hinihiling pa kundi ang manatili ka sa tabi ko.

"I choose you. And I'll choose you over and over and over. Without pause, without a doubt, in a heartbeat. I'll keep choosing you."

Hindi perpekto ang relationship natin - walang perfect relationship. Pero you know what? Perfect ka para sa akin. And together, we're perfect for each other. We balance each other out, we complement each other, we complete each other.

Mahal, I promise to always be here for you - sa good times and bad times, sa saya at lungkot, sa tagumpay at pagsubok. I promise to love you unconditionally, to support your dreams, to make you laugh when you're sad, and to hold your hand through everything life throws our way.

Thank you for being mine. Thank you for letting me be yours. Thank you for this beautiful love story that we're writing together. Every chapter with you is my favorite chapter.

"Mahal na mahal kita. Today, tomorrow, and forever. You are my always and my forever. My today, my tomorrow, and all of my tomorrows. Happy Valentine's Day, my love!"

Hindi sapat ang isang araw para ipakita ko kung gaano kita kamahal, kaya araw-araw, ipapakita ko sa'yo. But today, on this special day for lovers, gusto ko lang na mas maalala mo pa kung gaano ka kahalaga sa akin.

I love you more than words can express. I love you more than yesterday, but less than tomorrow. I love you to the moon and back, to infinity and beyond. I love you with all my heart, with all my soul, with every fiber of my being.

Forever yours,
George V
